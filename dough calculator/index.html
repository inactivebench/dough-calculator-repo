Assumptions

As you build your recipe using, here are some assumptions for your consideration:

  • Flour percentage is 100% and from this we base our calculations
  • The flour and water used for either poolish or Leaven are considered to be part of the flour and water weight in their respective fields
  • Both Poolish and Leaven have 100% hydration. (equal parts of flour and water)
